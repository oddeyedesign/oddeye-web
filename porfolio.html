Oni Clothing by Wismichu

Branding for a clothing brand that combines Japanese folklore elements with cyberpunk aesthetics. The logo is a simple modern design that features the japanese kanji for "Oni" (a type of demon). I also designed a collection of clothing that features illustrations of yokai masks and other japanese mythical creatures in a cyberpunk style.

Penguin Random House Spain: Young Readers Christmas Campaign

Some of the graphics I designed for Penguin Random House Spain (the whole project includes adaptations for various social media and websites), promoting some of their most popular titles for one of their Christmas campaigns, under the slogan: "This Christmas you deserve to live a great story". The campaign was aimed at young adult readers, so I used a modern yet classical eye-catching graphic style with very vibrang colors, without losing the Christmas essence that this campaign needed.

Primera Orden Games

Crafted a daring brand identity for a tiny indie videogame studio, evoking the nostalgic charm of retro pixel art mixed with the bold spirit of Soviet propaganda. Each team member was transformed into a digital illustration, their pixelized avatars adorning custom designed business cards.

TEAcup app

A personal project aimed to empower neurodivergent, disabled, and chronically ill people by providing them with a tool that allows them to hve full control over their limited energies in a simple visual way. Developed using the Double Diamond methodology in an exhaustive research process. Everything from the colors to the design style is designed to be user-friendly for the target audience. Project nominated by CEI for the 2024 MINI Design Awards on the "New Talents" category.

MindEat app

How can technology help reduce food waste? MindEat is an answer to that question. MindEat identifies expiring ingredients, and transforms them into irresistible deals, all seamlessly integrated into their digital menus. MindEat boasts a sleek and user-friendly interface tailored to the needs of the venue's staff.
